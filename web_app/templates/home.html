Indian Vehicle License Plate Recognition

A classical Digital Image Processing pipeline utilizing Auto-Canny edge detection, geometric filtering, and Heuristic OCR validation to extract license plates in uncontrolled environments.

Test the System Live

Adaptive Localization

Uses dynamic Auto-Canny thresholding to adjust to harsh lighting, shadows, and varying contrasts natively without requiring deep learning models.

Heuristic Fallbacks

Applies a Levenshtein-distance based state-code dictionary and whole-image OCR fallbacks to rescue edge cases and geometric failures.

Strict Regex Validation

Automatically cleans and validates text outputs against standard Indian (e.g., MH12AB1234) and Bharat (BH) series formatting rules.
